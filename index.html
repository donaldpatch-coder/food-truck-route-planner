# Sales Log ![Sales Log screenshot](../../assets/help/sales-log.png) Use Sales Log when the truck starts service. ## What To Do 1. Choose the location. 2. Click **Start Service**. 3. Enter sales as orders come in. 4. Add costs, best sellers, competitor count, and notes. 5. Choose whether to appear in the visible owner list for that location. 6. Click **Stop & Fill Time**. 7. Save the check-in. ## Why It Matters Saved check-ins power location history, reports, profit per visit, owner visibility, and future recommendations. ## Privacy Note Visible check-ins are optional. If the owner does not turn that option on, the app still saves their private sales history without listing them for other owners.
